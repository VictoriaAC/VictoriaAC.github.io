...About Cabbage

  1. Brussles sprouts are the same species of plant as cabbage, just a bud-forming variety (gemmifera)
  2. Brussels sprouts originated in Belgium, which is why they are named "brussels" sprouts not "brussel" sprouts.
  3. Broccoli, caulifower, kale, and kohrabi (among others) are also varieties of cabbage, cultived for their individual properties and appearances.
  4. Wild cabbage is native to Soutern and Western Europe.
  5. Members of the Brassica genus are known as cruciferous vegetables.
  6. Scott Robb from Palmer, Alaska holds the world record for the heaviest cabbage (138.25 lbs) and heaviest kohlrabi (96.9lbs).
